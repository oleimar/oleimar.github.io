Emeritus Professor

About me

I am active in the Ethology division of the Department of Zoology at Stockholm University. My research spans over a broad range, from evolutionary and game-theory modelling to animal behaviour experiments.

Interests

  • Game Theory in Biology
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Conflict and Cooperation
  • Mimicry and Aposematism
  • Developmental Plasticity
  • Genetic Polymorphism
  • Animal Personality
